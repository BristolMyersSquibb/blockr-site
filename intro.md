# Book a blockr intro session <Badge type="tip" text="free" />

Bring a real problem and we work through it with you in blockr in two free 30-minute
sessions.

## How it works

Session 1 is 30 minutes. We look at what you do today and where it slows you
down, sketch the blockr pipeline for it, and point you at the docs and examples
that fit your case. You leave with a plan.

Then you try it on your own data over the next week or two.

Session 2 is another 30 minutes, one or two weeks later. You show what you
built, we sort out whatever you got stuck on, and we agree what to do next. We
book it at the end of Session 1.

## Who it is for

- R users with a workflow they repeat or share across a team
- Analysts whose colleagues need to run or read the result without coding
- Educators who want a visual teaching tool
- Teams thinking about moving off bespoke Shiny or teal

You do not need to know blockr already. A concrete problem is enough to start.

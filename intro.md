# Book a blockr intro session <Badge type="tip" text="free" />

Bring a real problem and we work through it with you in blockr in two free 30-minute
sessions.

## How it works

<div class="intro-steps">
  <div class="intro-step">
    <span class="intro-num" style="--c:#0072B2">1</span>
    <div>
      <strong>Session 1, 30 minutes.</strong> We look at what you do today and
      where it slows you down, sketch the blockr pipeline for it, and point you
      at the docs and examples that fit your case. You leave with a plan. Then
      you try it on your own data over the next week or two.
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="intro-step">
    <span class="intro-num" style="--c:#009E73">2</span>
    <div>
      <strong>Session 2, 30 minutes, one or two weeks later.</strong> You show
      what you built, we sort out whatever you got stuck on, and we agree what
      to do next. We book it at the end of Session 1.
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

## Who it is for

- R users with a workflow they repeat or share across a team
- Analysts whose colleagues need to run or read the result without coding
- Educators who want a visual teaching tool
- Teams thinking about moving off bespoke Shiny

You do not need to know blockr already. A concrete problem is enough to start.

the prep we need.

## What to bring

A problem you actually have, with some example data if you can. Bring whatever
you use for it now, whether that is a script, a spreadsheet, or a Shiny app.

If you are not sure blockr fits your case, book anyway. Working that out is part
of Session 1.
